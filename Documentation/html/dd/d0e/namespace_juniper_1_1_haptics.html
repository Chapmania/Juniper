enum Juniper.Haptics.HapticExpression
strong

Pre-canned feedback patterns. These are defined by iOS's Taptic feedback system, which is the only feedback system that is readily available to us. It's fairly useful, so we reimplement it on Android.

Enumerator
None 

No interaction, do nothing.

SelectionChange 

A light tap to indicate a change in selection.

Light 

A light tap, slightly stronger than SelectionChange.

Medium 

A medium tap, stronger than Light/

Heavy 

A heavy thud, stronger than Medium.

Warning 

A pattern of feedback pulses that evokes "Warning".

Error 

A pattern of feedback pulses that evokes "Error".

Success 

A pattern of feedback pulses that evokes "Success".

Click 

A button press and release.

Press 

The first half of a click.

Release 

The second half of a click.
