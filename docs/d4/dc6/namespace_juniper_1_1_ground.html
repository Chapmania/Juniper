enum Juniper.Ground.GroundMeshVisualization
strong

Different ways to visualize the ground from within an app.

Enumerator
None 

Do not perform any ground rendering.

Occluded 

Render the ground with a material that is transparent to the background camera feed, but opaque to other AR objects.

Debug 

Render the ground with a material that is always visible, and may include debugging information about terrain feature points.
