◆ PlayTaptic()

void PlayTaptic ( unsigned int  index)

Play one of the Taptic feedback patterns:

  • 1 -> selection changed
  • 2 -> light impact
  • 3 -> medium impact
  • 4 -> heavy impact
  • 5 -> warning notification
  • 6 -> error notification
  • 7 -> success notification.
